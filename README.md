# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, and regional performance using Power BI.

The objective was to transform raw transactional data into a client-ready business dashboard with actionable insights.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- Business Analytics

## Dashboard Features
- Total Revenue KPI
- Revenue Trend Over Time
- Top 10 Products by Revenue
- Top 10 Countries by Revenue
- Interactive Country Filter

## Key Insights
- Total revenue generated from the dataset is $8.91M.
- The United Kingdom contributes the largest share of revenue, making it the strongest market.
- A small group of products accounts for a large portion of sales.
- Revenue shows a steady upward trend over time, indicating growing demand.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on the United Kingdom market to maximize revenue.
- Promote high-performing products through targeted campaigns.
- Explore opportunities to expand in underperforming regions.
- Use demand trends for improved inventory planning.

## Dashboard Access
The interactive dashboard can be accessed by downloading the Power BI file from this repository and opening it in Power BI Desktop.
