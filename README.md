# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes an e-commerce sales dataset to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, and regional performance.

The goal was to transform raw transactional data into a client-ready business dashboard that supports data-driven decision making.

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Data Visualization
- Data Cleaning
- Business Analytics

---

## Business Problem
Businesses often struggle to quickly identify revenue trends, high-performing products, and regional market performance from large transactional datasets.

This dashboard helps decision-makers monitor performance and identify opportunities for growth.

---

## Dashboard Features
- Total Revenue KPI
- Revenue Trend Over Time
- Top 10 Products by Revenue
- Top 10 Countries by Revenue
- Interactive Country Filter

---

## Key Insights
- Total revenue generated from the dataset is $8.91M
- The United Kingdom contributes the largest share of revenue
- A small number of products drive a large portion of sales
- Revenue shows a steady upward trend, indicating growing demand

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on the United Kingdom, the strongest revenue market
- Promote top-performing products to maximize sales
- Investigate underperforming regions for potential expansion
- Use demand trends to improve inventory planning
